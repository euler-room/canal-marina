About

Canal Marina is on the south side of the Lake Washington Ship Canal. It is next to the Burke-Gilman Trail and only steps from Seattle Pacific University. The marina can be found between the Fremont and Ballard bridges and is a close hop to the Fremont Restaurant and Entertainment District and the historic Queen Anne Neighborhood. It is only minutes to downtown Seattle, the University of Washington, Gas Works Park, and the Space Needle.

Green Initiative

Canal Marina is committed to keeping our water and environment clean while providing facilities for recreational boaters and live-aboard tenants. Protecting our waters, marine life, fish and waterfowl from boat and marina pollutants is a major goal for us. Our staff, along with every boater, work together to keep the marina and environment clean.
